Featured Hotels in Kandy District

See more

Earl's Regency Hotel Kandy

Earl's Regency Hotel is a luxurious 5-star hotel located a 5-minute drive from Kandy Railway Station. It offers spacious accommodation with a spa, an outdoor pool and free parking.

The Tourmaline Kandy

The Tourmaline is located on Anniewatte Mountain, 1.5 km from the famous Temple of the Tooth. It offers a pool, well-appointed rooms with bathtubs and free Wi-Fi in public areas.

Serene Garden Hotel Kandy

Overlooking Kandy Lake, this hotel offers rooms with a private balcony. Surrounded by tropical greenery, it features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi in its public areas.

The Planter's Hotel Kandy

The Planter's Hotel is conveniently located 400 metres from Kandy Lake and just a 15-minute walk from downtown Kandy. The 4-storey hotel offers a restaurant and complimentary parking spaces.

Randholee Luxury Resort Kandy

Presenting beautiful views of Alagalla Mountains, Randholee Luxury Resort has an infinity pool, free Wi-Fi in public area and Ayurvedic massage..
