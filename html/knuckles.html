Knuckles mountain range

Knuckles mountain range is located within the district of Kandy in Sri Lanka. Its appearance resembles a set of knuckles of a clenched fist. With the highest point about 6,000 ft above sea level the Knuckles range is a widespread region covering about 155 sq. km. Knuckles mountain range is located within the district of Kandy in Sri Lanka. Its appearance resembles a set of knuckles of a clenched fist. With the highest point about 6,000 ft above sea level the Knuckles range is a widespread region covering about 155 sq. km. A wide range of rare and endemic flora and fauna makes this wilderness area a storehouse of rich biodiversity. There are 31 species of mammals recorded in the Knuckles wilderness. The Knuckles range is also a semi evergreen forest. The entire region is also unbelievably diverse in terms of climate and terrain. You could easily trek through the dense forests. Panoramic views of misty mountains, stunning valleys, ancient rock formations, beautiful animal and plant life, and hospitable people make the Knuckles range a must visit place in Sri Lanka.
